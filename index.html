CHICKEN BIRYANI

ingredients

Chicken biryani is a beloved culinary masterpiece that has captivated taste buds worldwide. This dish is characterized by its layers of fluffy basmati rice, succulent chicken, and a rich blend of spices, making it a staple in many households and a highlight at celebrations and gatherings.

  • Basmati rice - 100 grams
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas) - 150 grams
  • Onion - 50 grams
  • Tomato 50 grams
  • Ginger-garlic paste 10 grams (1 tsp)
  • Green chilies - 5 grams (1-2 pieces)
  • Curd/Yogurt - 50 ml (4 tbsp)
  • Ghee or oil - 20 ml (2 tbsp)
  • Biryani masala - 5 grams (1 tsp)
  • Turmeric powder - 1 gram (¼ tsp)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Whole spices (bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, mace) - small portion
  • Mint and coriander leaves - few sprigs for garnish
  • Lime/lemon juice - optional, ½ tsp
  • Saffron soaked in milk - optional, adds aroma and colorSummary

instruction

  1. Chicken: Typically drumsticks or thighs for juiciness and flavor
  2. Basmati Rice: Long-grain rice is preferred for its fragrance and fluffy texture
  3. Yogurt: Used for marinating the chicken and adding creaminess
  4. Onions: Thinly sliced and often caramelized for sweetness and depth
  5. Tomatoes: Provide moisture and a subtle tang
  6. Ginger and Garlic Paste: Essential for aromatic flavor.
  7. Herbs: Fresh mint and coriander leaves for freshness and aroma
  8. Saffron: Soaked in warm milk or water to add color and fragrance
  9. Ghee or Oil: Used for frying onions and cooking the biryani layers
Chicken biryani can be a delicious and nutritious dish, offering health benefits when prepared mindfully, but it also has potential drawbacks due to its calorie and fat content. Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful Indian rice dish made with marinated chicken, aromatic spices, and basmati rice, often garnished with fried onions, fresh herbs, and served with raita or salad.
